EAST AFRICAN GLOBAL WARMING SUMMIT 2023

EAC summit has been around for a decade now and the change and contribution it has brought to member countries is immense. EAC takes place annually in the month of March and there have been a record of 200 talks from more than 100 professionlas around the world. Since the summit started over a decade ago, we have always recruited interested speakers from different countries. You can always reach out to us if you are interested in any way possible. Every donation will always go towards the betterness of the climate crisis in africa

For clarifications about the EA Global Warming Summit 2023, contact us:
eac2023@african.edu
